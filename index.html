📖 About Eid ul Adha

🕌 Significance

Eid ul Adha marks the strong faith of Prophet Ibrahim (A.S.), who was willing to sacrifice his son in obedience to Allah.

🤝 Values

The festival teaches sacrifice, devotion, generosity, and caring for others in society.

🌍 Celebration

Muslims celebrate with special prayers, family gatherings, sharing food, and helping the needy.

Eid ul Adha is a time to reflect on faith, strengthen relationships, and spread kindness. It reminds us that true happiness lies in giving and sharing with others.
